ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Corean and I am a web developer based in Austin, TX. I love developing static websites and dynamic web apps. My background is in performance art. I love merging my eye for aesthetic and precision from my performance background into web development.

I find the process to be very similiar to choreographing a performance routine: Taking the idea/concept, wireframing/mockups (writing the choreography), coding and perfecting (rehearsing), then finally revealing the site (the debut performance).
